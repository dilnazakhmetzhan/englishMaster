It all started with two smart students studying English

The network of language schools was founded in 2021. We provide professional English courses for all levels.We have international qualifications in the field of teaching, as well as experience in teaching children and adults in different countries of the world. The use of communicative methodology in the study of foreign languages is the main focus of English Master.

Every year we hold summer language camps for schoolchildren of all ages, theater performances and much more. We provide English language courses to corporate clients.

We believe in:

  • the power of native-speaker training
  • interactive, personalized lessons
  • client-centric training
  • quality customer service
  • empowered teachers and students
  • the transformative power of language
